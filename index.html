Anil Ozturk is a Senior Machine Learning Engineer at Eatron Technologies. He has BSc. in Mechatronics Engineering at Yildiz Technical University and completed MSc. in Computer Engineering at Istanbul Technical University.

Regardless of the field of implementation, Anil is interested in and enthusiastically working on all subjects that fall within the scope of machine learning, deep learning and reinforcement learning. He follows Kaggle actively and try to spend as much time as possible for local and global competitions. He currently chooses GBDTs and linear models for traditional machine learning, PyTorch and Tensorflow (mostly PyTorch) for deep learning, Ray-RLLib and stable-baselines for reinforcement learning.
